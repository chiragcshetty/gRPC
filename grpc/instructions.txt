Troubleshoot:
If port is unavailable check: netstat -nlp|grep <enter port no>
And kill the process using the port using: kill -9 <pid>

Or  simply use: fuser -k <port_no>/tcp
eg: fuser -k 8008/tcp 8007/tcp 8006/tcp
